# Aevryn Terms Of Service

> Draft for attorney review before public launch.

These draft terms describe intended product boundaries for Aevryn. They are not a substitute for legal review.

---

# Acceptable Use

Users may use Aevryn for lawful creative, educational, research, and production workflows.

Users may not use Aevryn to attack the platform, violate law, infringe rights, distribute malware, send spam, or abuse other users.

---

# User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

* having rights to upload and process their content
* maintaining account security
* reviewing outputs before relying on them
* complying with applicable laws

---

# Accounts

Users must provide accurate account information where required.

Aetherra Labs may suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms or create security risk.

---

# Payments

Payments are future scope.

Future paid plans must define billing, renewal, cancellation, refund, and tax terms before launch.

---

# Intellectual Property

Users retain ownership of uploaded stories, project data, generated canon, and exports.

Aetherra Labs retains ownership of Aevryn software, platform code, product design, trademarks, and service infrastructure.

Users grant Aetherra Labs only the limited rights needed to operate Aevryn for their account.

---

# Service Availability

Aevryn may change, pause, or discontinue features.

Aetherra Labs should make reasonable efforts to preserve user access and data export paths, but no service can guarantee uninterrupted availability.

---

# Termination

Users may stop using Aevryn.

Aetherra Labs may terminate access for violations, legal obligations, security threats, or nonpayment once payments exist.

---

# Warranty Disclaimer

Aevryn is provided as-is unless a final attorney-reviewed agreement says otherwise.

Outputs may require user review.

---

# Liability Limitation

Liability limits must be drafted by counsel before public launch.

---

# Governing Law

Governing law must be selected by counsel before public launch.
