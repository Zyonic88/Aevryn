# Aevryn User Rights

> Built by **Aetherra Labs**

Plain-English trust promises for Aevryn users.

---

# Your Story

You own it.

We do not.

Uploading a manuscript, chapter, screenplay, comic script, or worldbuilding note does not transfer ownership to Aetherra Labs.

---

# Your Canon

The canon Aevryn builds from your story belongs to you.

Aevryn is a tool for understanding your work, not a claim over your work.

---

# Your Exports

Exports generated from your project belong to you.

You should be able to use them in your creative workflow, subject to the rights you already have in the underlying story.

---

# Your Data

You should be able to export your data.

You should be able to delete your projects.

Deletion must remove story data from active Aevryn-owned storage. If production backups retain deleted data for a disclosed recovery window, that must be explained clearly.

---

# AI Training

Off by default.

Opt-in only.

Aetherra Labs does not train on user stories without explicit opt-in.

---

# Privacy

Employees do not browse customer stories by default.

Access must be limited, justified, and auditable where technically possible.

---

# Transparency

Aevryn should explain what it knows and why.

It should show evidence-backed canon, not hidden guesses.

It should not invent hidden data.

---

# Company Principle

```text
Your work belongs to you.
```
