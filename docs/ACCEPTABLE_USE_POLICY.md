# Aevryn Acceptable Use Policy

> Draft for attorney review before public launch.

This policy separates product safety from content ownership.

---

# Allowed Uses

Aevryn may be used for lawful work such as:

* fiction
* novels
* web novels
* screenplays
* comics
* mature novels
* academic work
* private worldbuilding
* continuity tracking
* production planning

---

# Not Allowed

Users may not use Aevryn for:

* copyright infringement
* uploading content they do not have rights to process
* malware
* spam
* phishing
* platform abuse
* attempts to attack, scan, overload, or bypass Aevryn systems
* illegal material
* attempts to extract other users' data
* evading security controls

---

# Mature Fiction

Aevryn is content-aware, not content-opinionated.

Lawful mature fiction is not automatically prohibited. Mature and explicit projects may require classification, UI warnings, provider restrictions, or future moderation handling.

---

# Enforcement

Aetherra Labs may limit, suspend, or terminate access for violations, security risk, legal obligations, or platform abuse.

Enforcement processes should be documented before public launch.
